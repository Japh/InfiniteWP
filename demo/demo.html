  1. 1/12List of Websites

    Once added, you can access all your websites from here. Organize your sites into groups for easy managing.

  2. 2/12Process queue

    All operations you initiate will be queued here for processing.Once queued, you can continue with your other operations without having to wait for it to complete.

  3. 3/12Main navigation

    This is the app's main navigation.

  4. 4/12Updates Views

    You can view updates by websites or by items.

  5. 5/12Update

    Apply all available updates, or update items by websites or update them individually. You can also hide update notification for items.

  6. 6/12Select Websites

    To manage plugins & themes you must first select the websites. You can select websites by group or individually or both.

  7. 7/12Load Info

    After selecting the websites, you can load information of all plugins / themes from the selected sites or search for specific plugin / theme and load their data alone. You can activate, deactivate and delete items.

  8. 8/12Install Plugins/Themes

    You can bulk install plugins / themes here. You can install items from the WP repository or upload them from your computer or from a url.

  9. 9/12Install Theme

    Install themes, view their details and add to favorites here.

  10. 10/12Backups

    Create backups of sites & they will be listed here. You can download, delete & restore them.

  11. 11/12Activity Log

    All activities in the app will be logged here. You can check statuses of operations and also filter them within a date range.

  12. 12/12Reload Data

    Fetch real-time data from all websites.
